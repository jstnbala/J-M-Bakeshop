ABOUT US

Whisking Up Delightful Dreams: Unveiling the Sweet Story of J&M Bakeshop


Imagine a place where the tantalizing aroma of freshly baked customized cakes envelops you as soon as you step inside. Welcome to J&M Bakeshop , where every delectable treat is crafted with love and passion, straight from the hearts of a remarkable family.

J&M Bakeshop is the brand name of the business, originated from each letter, in particular, “J” and “M” in the name that represents the initial letter of each member of the family. These names are inspired by Maricel, Jerold, Janine, and Justin. The term “bakeshop” was added at the end because producing mouthwatering customized cakes for the public grew into our passion for life.

Since 2018, J&M Bakeshop has been the go-to destination for personalized cake creations in Marikina City . Though we're currently operating without a physical store, yet our dream is to expand soon. At J&M Bakeshop, it's all about crafting home-made cakes that perfectly match your desires . Your imagination is our secret ingredient!
